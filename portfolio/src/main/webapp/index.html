Free Speech on the Internet

This is a collection of papers and drafts I wrote for one of my favorite courses at Berkeley: college writing on technology and society. In a world where technology is prevalent, and especially as someone engaged in the tech space, it's important to recognize the effects it has on the world. Mostly, it was just really fun and interesting to read deeply into a lot of issues that really fascinated me, such as the NBA / Daryl Morey / Hong Kong / China controversey.

NP-Hard Algorithmic Problem

Created a randomized algorithm to solve the provided NP-hard problem. The problem consists of finding a "spanning" tree that minimizes average pairwise distance. Caveat: the tree doesn't have to span all the vertices. As long as all vertices are in or have a neighbor in the tree, you're good. Implemented a gradient-descent algorithm in Python to find an approximately good solution for small, medium, and large inputs.
